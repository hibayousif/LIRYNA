# LIRYNA
The project, LIRYNA, is a specialized "Aesthetic Wellness &amp; Brew" web application. Unlike standard cafe websites, LIRYNA is designed as an interactive tool where users can customize their drinks and self-care rituals in a way that aligns with their health needs and aesthetic preferences.
# ✦ LIRYNA — Aesthetic Wellness & Brew ✦

**LIRYNA** is an aesthetic web experience that blends **wellness, self-care, beauty, and café culture** into one calming digital sanctuary.

Designed for the modern woman, LIRYNA lets users **craft their vibe** by creating a personalized drink and receiving a small beauty gift — turning a simple order into a soothing ritual.

> Your drink honors your health. Your ritual honors your soul.

---

## 🌿 Concept

LIRYNA is more than a café interface.  
It is an **interactive wellness flow** focused on mood, allergies, glow, and aesthetic comfort.

Users move through a gentle 4-step journey:
1. Choose a drink base
2. Pick a milk alternative
3. Add a fresh bakery item
4. Receive a beauty glow gift
